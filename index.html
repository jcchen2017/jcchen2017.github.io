About me

Hello. I am a post-doc at Fudan University, working with Weiguo Gao and Ke Wei. Before that, I completed my Ph.D. in School of Physics at Beijing Institute of Technology, where I worked with Prof. Yulong Liu. I received B.E. in School of Information and Communication Engineering from Nanjing Institute of Technology.
