About Me

I'm an Assistant Professor in School of Mathematics at East China University of Science and Technology (ECUST).

Before joining ECUST, I was a postdoctoral researcher in Fudan University, where I was fortunate to work with Prof. Weiguo Gao and Prof. Ke Wei.

Education

  • Ph.D. School of Physics at Beijing Institute of Technology, under the supervision of Prof. Yulong Liu.

  • B.E. School of Information and Communication Engineering from Nanjing Institute of Technology.

Research Interests

My research interests lie in mathematics of data science, machine learning and signal processing, including compressed sensing, reinforcement learning and distributed optimization.
