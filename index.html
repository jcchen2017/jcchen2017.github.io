About Me

I'm a postdoctoral researcher in the School of Data Science at Fudan University, advised by Prof. Weiguo Gao and Prof. Ke Wei. Previously, I completed my Ph.D. in the School of Physics at Beijing Institute of Technology, under the supervision of Prof. Yulong Liu. Prior to attending Beijing Institute of Technology, I received B.E. in the School of Information and Communication Engineering from Nanjing Institute of Technology.

Research Interests

My research interests lie in mathematics of data science, machine learning and signal processing, including compressed sensing, reinforcement learning and distributed optimization.
